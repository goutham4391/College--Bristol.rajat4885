Campus tours

Our campus tours provide an opportunity for prospective undergraduate students to learn more about the University of Bristol.

Book your place

Join our current students for a tour around the Clifton campus. Tours will give you an overview of central University buildings and the opportunity to ask a current student about their experience studying at Bristol. 

Book your place today

 

Essential information before you visit:

  • Tours last for up to two hours and include some steps and hills.
  • Accessible tours are available for visitors with restricted mobility or wheelchair users. Accessibilty requirements can be provided upon booking.
  • Most of the tour is outside so please dress for the weather and terrain.  
  • If you would like somebody to accompany you on the tour, please book a place for one guest. Group numbers will be restricted to keep you and our community safe.   
  • Please note the route does not include a tour of any accommodation.
  • Face coverings and social distancing are no longer required as per government guidelines and University policy. Visitors are welcome to wear face coverings if they prefer.
Please get in touch if you have any questions prior to your visit. Email: choosebristol-ug@bristol.ac.uk    

Our tours offer you an overview of the University campus. The tours are not subject specific, so you won't be able to visit academic departments or meet with admissions tutors.

Disabled access

The University has teamed up with AccessAble to provide you with detailed information about the accessibility of our facilities.
