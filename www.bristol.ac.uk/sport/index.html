Inclusivity statement

The University of Bristol is an inclusive and caring campus where diversity is celebrated, antisocial attitudes and behaviours are challenged, and where there is no place for racism, discrimination, harassment, or abuse. We are committed to creating an inspiring community where all members, from all backgrounds, have a positive and enriching experience. We are continuously working to ensure that we are a place where everyone feels able to be themselves and to be their best, and we encourage everybody in our community to be an active bystander and speak up if they see or hear something that's not right.

Stand Up Speak Out is our campaign to encourage students, staff and our wider community to take action against bullying, harassment and other forms of unacceptable behaviour they may experience or witness.
